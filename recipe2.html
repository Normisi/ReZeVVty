Fried Chicken

45 mins • Tricky

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, etc.)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Oil for deep frying (vegetable or canola)
