Cucumber salad

5 mins • Super easy

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh cucumbers (thinly sliced)
  • ½ small red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (apple cider or white)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill (optional)
